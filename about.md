# About me

I am a pre-final year undergrad at the [Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IIT_Jodhpur), India.

# Why did I start writing

Today's teaching methods contain more and more jargon and less and less clarity. Many concepts may seem difficult at the outset but can be understood quite simply using appropriate analogies, clarifications and stories. This prompted me to start writing a blog which explains machine learning concepts effectively and easily.
